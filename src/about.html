About Us!

NAS is an Arabic word that means ‘people.’ You might wonder why we chose a word in Arabic to reflect our brand. To us NAS is not just a word. It is a reflection of our brand’s most valuable asset: our people! The people who are well-trained to provide support in an efficient and effective manner.

NAS has been providing digital, easy-to-use solutions and transformations through its wide array of services for over 20+ years in different regions including but not limited to KSA, Oman, and Egypt.

Mission

At NAS, our mission is to empower individuals and organizations through innovative digital solutions and unwavering support. With over 20 years of expertise, we strive to deliver excellence to our diverse clientele, serving both local and international regions. We are committed to providing cost-effective and efficient services that exceed expectations, ensuring the success and satisfaction of our customers.

Vision

NAS envisions a future where digital solutions seamlessly enhance lives and businesses worldwide. Rooted in our Arabic heritage, we believe in the power of people and their ability to drive meaningful change. Our vision is to continue leading the industry with cutting-edge technologies and transformative services, enriching communities and fostering growth on a global scale. As we embark on this journey, our focus remains steadfast on our most valuable asset: our people, who embody the spirit of NAS and drive our commitment to excellence.

Fast. Reliable. Available 24/7

Our motto is: ‘Always improving, innovating, excelling.’  And in that sense we worked hard over the years to partner up with the best service providers. We made sure our team grew exponentially to maintain the promise we offered since our very first beginning. When you choose NAS you are guaranteed to have the highest quality of Digital solutions with the most affordable prices available, and a 24/7 support team dedicated to you.
